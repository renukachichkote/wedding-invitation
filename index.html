About Us

We are brought together through an arranged marriage - a beautiful blend of tradition and destiny. Though the journey began with the help of families, we've grown to appreciate each other. Our story is one of mutual respect, shared values, and the excitement of building a new life. And we're excited to share this special chapter of our lives with you.

Events

7th May

Haldi and Sangeet

5pm - 10pm

Join us for the vibrant Haldi ceremony where turmeric paste will be applied to both the bride and groom. This auspicious pre-wedding ritual is believed to bless the couple with prosperity and purification before their wedding day. The Sangeet is a joyous occasion where family members and friends showcase their talents through choreographed performances. Come prepared to enjoy delicious food, refreshments, and be ready to dance the night away!

8th May

Wedding Ceremony

11am - 1pm

The sacred wedding ceremony will take place as per traditional customs, where Vijaylaxmi and Viresh will exchange vows and begin their journey together as a married couple. This auspicious event will include all the ceremonial rituals that symbolize the union of not just two individuals but also their families.

8th May

Reception

1pm - 4pm

We cordially invite you to join us for celebration as we honor the newlyweds. The reception will feature a formal lunch and an opportunity to bless the couple as they begin their new life together. Your presence will make this joyous occasion even more special as we celebrate the union of Vijaylaxmi and Viresh.
