Events

15th August

Mehndi

1pm - 5pm

It's one of the oldest traditional pre-wedding ceremony where the bride and groom attend the event together and a professional mehndi artist applies mehndi to the bride's hands and feet. This will be having a celebratory festival feel to it with people wearing vibrant clothes and dancing, singing, playing games and a lot more.

16th August

Sangeet

6pm - 10pm

A fun-filled evening of music and dance performances by friends and family celebrating the upcoming union. There will be delicious food, drinks, and lots of dancing!

17th August

Wedding Ceremony

11am - 3pm

The main wedding ceremony where we will take our vows and begin our journey together as a married couple. Traditional rituals will be performed according to our customs.

17th August

Reception

7pm - 11pm

Join us for the reception dinner and celebration following the ceremony. There will be speeches, toasts, dinner, and dancing to celebrate our new beginning.
